Homemade Cookie Dough Recipe

Description

This homemade cookie dough is a delightful treat that captures the essence of classic cookies without the need for baking. With its rich, buttery flavor and a perfect balance of sweetness, it's a nostalgic indulgence that can be enjoyed straight from the bowl or used as a topping for ice cream. Safe to eat raw, this cookie dough is a versatile dessert that brings joy to both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/8 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (heat treated)
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Spread the flour on a baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees Farenheit for 5 minutes
  2. Mix the dough in a small bowl, cream together the softened butter and brown sugar until smooth
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract
  4. Add the heat-treated flour and a pinch of salt to the butter mixture
  5. Stir in the milk until the dough reaches your desired consistency
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips
  7. Enjoy your edible cookie dough immediately
