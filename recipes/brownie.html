Homemade Brownie Recipe

Description

These homemade brownies are a chocolate lover's dream, with a rich, fudgy texture that melts in your mouth. Each bite is packed with deep chocolate flavor, making them the perfect indulgence for any occasion. Whether enjoyed warm from the oven or cooled to perfection, these brownies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth and bring a smile to your face.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Vvnilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix together the dry and wet ingredients in two separate bowls
  2. Combine the wet and dry ingredients
  3. Pour the batter into an 8x8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper
  4. Transfer to pan
  5. Bake at 325 degrees Farenheit for 45 minutes
  6. Use toothpick to check when finished
