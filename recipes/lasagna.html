Crockpot Lasagna Recipe

Description

This crockpot lasagna is a comforting and hearty dish, featuring layers of rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and gooey cheese. Perfect for family gatherings or a cozy night in, this recipe brings the warmth of Italian home cooking to your table.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 2&1/2 cups tomato pasta sauce
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 9 lasanga noodles (not oven ready)
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cups parmasean cheese

Instructions

  1. In a medium skillet cook Italian sausage until completely browned
  2. Stir in pasta sauce and salt
  3. Lightly grease the crockpot
  4. Spread a little bit of sauce on the bottom of the crockpot
  5. Place 3 lasagna noodles in the bottom
  6. Spread sauce on noodles and top with cheese
  7. Repeat layers 2 more times
  8. Cook on low for 3 hours
