Crack Dip Recipe

Description

This crack dip is a creamy, cheesy delight that combines the rich flavors of cream cheese, cheddar, and ranch seasoning with crispy bacon bits. Perfect for parties or game day, it's a crowd-pleaser that pairs well with chips or veggies.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese, softened
  • Ranch Seasoning Mix
  • Real bacon bits
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Green onions

Instructions

  1. Preheay oven to 400 degrees Farenheit
  2. Mix together the cream cheese, ranch, bacon, sour cream and part of the cheddar cheese by hand or mixer
  3. Put the mixture into a 8x8 baking dish
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. During the last 5 minutes, remove the foil and top with the remaining cheddar cheese on top.
  6. Bake a few more minutes until the cheese is melted
  7. Garnish with green olives or chives, and serve warm
