Homemade Mac n Cheese Recipe

Description

This homemade mac n cheese is a creamy and comforting dish that brings the classic flavors of childhood to your table. With its rich, cheesy sauce enveloping perfectly cooked pasta, it's a delightful indulgence that warms the soul.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1&1/2 cups milk, any kind
  • 3/4 cup half and half
  • 2&1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees Farenheit and lightly grease an 8' square baking dish
  2. Cook the macaroni to al dente, according to package instructions
  3. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over the medium heat.
  4. Blend in the flour salt, and pepper.
  5. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Slowly add the milk and half and half, stirring constantly, and cook over medium-low heat for a few minutes until the mixture has thickened like the consistency of gravy, about 5-7 minutes
  7. Remove from heat and stir in 1 cup shredded cheese, stirring just until melted
  8. Add the cooked macaronu noodles and toss to coat
  9. Pour half of the pasta mixture into the prepared baking dish
  10. Sprinkle 1/2 cup cheese over the top.
  11. Add remaining pasta and sprinkle with remaining cheese
  12. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is melted on top
