Apple Cinnamon Cake Recipe

Description

This apple cinnamon cake is the perfect blend of sweet, spiced, and comforting. Packed with tender chunks of fresh apples and warm notes of cinnamon, each bite delivers a moist crumb and cozy flavor that feels like fall in dessert form. Whether enjoyed with a cup of coffee or served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, this cake is a timeless treat for any season.

Ingredients

  • 1 package white cake mix (2 layer)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 (21oz) can apple pie filling
  • Glaze
  • 1 cup of confectioners' sugar
  • 1-1&1/2 tbsp milk

Instructions

  1. combine first 5 ingredients in a large bowl with a mixer until smooth
  2. Pour the dough into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish
  3. Spoon the apple pie filling on top of the dough, covering the entire surface
  4. Bake at 350 degrees Farenheit for 40 minutes
  5. Make the glaze with milk and confectioner's sugar and sprinkle over the cooled cake
  6. Enjoy!
