Homemade Cookie Recipe

Description

These homemade cookies are the perfect blend of comfort and indulgence, with a golden-brown exterior and a soft, chewy center that melts in your mouth. Packed with rich flavor and a hint of nostalgia in every bite, they're just the thing to satisfy your sweet tooth or share with loved ones. Whether enjoyed warm from the oven or saved for a special treat, these cookies bring the warmth of home-baking to life.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup salted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup favorite chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  2. Cream together the butter, granulated sugar and brown sugar until light in color and fluffy in texture.
  3. Scrape the sides of the boal and add in the egg and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  4. Add in the flour, baking sode and salt. Mix until a soft but not sticky dough forms.
  5. Fold in your favoirte chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop the dough out into 1 1/2 tbsp portions and arrange on your cookie sheet.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes or until the cookies have spread and are just beginning to turn golden brown on the edges.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire cooling rack.
