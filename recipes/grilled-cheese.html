Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

This homemade grilled cheese is the ultimate comfort food classic—crispy, golden-buttered bread enveloping a gooey, melty center of perfectly melted cheese. Each bite delivers a satisfying crunch followed by rich, creamy flavor that warms you from the inside out. Simple, nostalgic, and irresistibly delicious, it’s a timeless favorite that never goes out of style.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or your choice)
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • Butter for spreading
  • Optional: tomato slices, bacon, or other fillings

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in the skillet.
  4. Add cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.
  5. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side until golden and cheese is melted.
  7. Remove from skillet, cut in half, and serve hot.
