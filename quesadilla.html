Quesadilla Recipe

Description

These homemade quesadillas are a warm, melty fusion of flavor and simplicity—crispy golden tortillas filled with perfectly melted cheese and your favorite savory fillings. Lightly toasted to perfection, they offer a satisfying crunch on the outside with a rich, gooey interior that’s bursting with comforting, bold flavor. Whether served as a quick snack or a hearty meal, they’re a crowd-pleasing classic that hits the spot every time.

Ingredients

  • # of flour tortillas based on serving size
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your choice) per tortilla
  • additional fillings (optional): cooked chicken, beans, onions, bell peppers
  • olive oil for cooking
  • Salsa or sour cream for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add olive oil.
  2. Place tortilla in the skillet, sprinkle half of the cheese on one half of the tortilla.
  3. Add additional toppings if using, then fold the tortilla over.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and cheese is melted.
  5. Repeat
  6. Cut into wedges and serve with salsa or sour cream.
